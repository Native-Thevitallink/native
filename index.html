who we are

Native - The farmers Vital Link

Native has been established in the year 2023. Here at Native , we aim to uplift the backbone of our economy- The Farmers. Together, we cultivate success while maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community growth. Native strives to increase the agricultural rate of India and prevent migration of farmers. The main focus of our organization is towards making the youth realize, agriculture is as responsible as other professions & its importance by promoting tourism and making them feel the authenticity and peace of mind provided by villages and agriculture and assisting the farmers to directly sell their produce by eliminating the middlemen.

Services We Offer-

Tourism

"Journey through Farmers Enclave A Tapestry of Cultures and Wonders"

let NATIVE be your guide as you traverse stunning vistas, connect with welcoming locals, and collect memories to last a lifetime. It will be your backstage pass to uncover hidden gems, Your journey will be your compass to navigate the rich history, exquisite cuisine, exciting activities to serene moments of tranquility, and warm hospitality that await your exploration!

Empowering Farmers

“Direct from Farm to You”

Assisting the farmers in a way that their produce reaches directly to the end consumers by providing them a platform to connect with consumers (restaurants & small outlets) , and eliminating the chain of middlemen involved, helping them earn the revenue they deserve.
